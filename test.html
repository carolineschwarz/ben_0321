Benjamin Yates

I'm Benjamin Yates, an audio maker from the United Kingdom, now living in Berlin.
I produce radio documentaries, sound art and compositions, at the moment am focussed on experimenting with the application and representation of memory in audio. I'm interested in finding unique stories that help articulate that.

In the past year I've produced audio pieces for Deutschlandfunk Kultur, alongside the Field Recordings and Cities and Memory podcasts.
